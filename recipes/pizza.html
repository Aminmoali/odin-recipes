Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Like the all-American chains that flourish from sea to shining sea, pepperoni is a relatively recent innovation and one born in the United States

It first appeared in New York around 1919, though some pizza historians believe that the spicy dry sausage was first baked onto a pie a year later in New Haven, Connecticut.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Instant Yeast
  • salt
  • Warm Water
  • Olive Oli
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Red Pepper flakes
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Oregano Sprigs

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast and make the dough.
  2. Let dough rise.
  3. Roll out the dough.
  4. Transfer to baking sheet.
  5. Add toppings to pepperoni pizza.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes.
